A website that can quickly set up video recording points

Inspired by the fact that more and more people in today’s society like to watch long-form YouTube storytelling series videos, but cannot watch more than 20 hours of videos in one go, so it provides them with the opportunity to rely on this platform to record.

Why not use the playlist function provided by YouTube?

Since the original idea was to create a functional website with one-click projection to smart TVs, due to considerations of time and ability,
I have not yet conducted in-depth research (I am a student who recently took the 學測)
